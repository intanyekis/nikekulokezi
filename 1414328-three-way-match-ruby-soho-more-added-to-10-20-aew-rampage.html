Three-Way Match, Ruby Soho, More Added To 10/20 AEW Rampage

Multiple matches have been added to the October 20 episode of AEW Rampage.

As announced on the October 18 episode of AEW Dynamite, John Silver, Kip Sabian, and Brother Zay will clash in a three-way bout. The winner will earn a shot at Orange Cassidy’s AEW International Championship at AEW Battle of the Belts VIII.

Plus, Claudio Castagnoli and Wheeler Yuta will compete, and Skye Blue will take on Ruby Soho.

Updated Card For 10/20 AEW Rampage

The updated card for the October 20 episode of AEW Rampage is as follows:

  • Mexico’s Pound-For-Pound Crown: Mistico vs. Rocky Romero
  • Claudio Castagnoli & Wheeler Yuta in action
  • Skye Blue vs. Ruby Soho
  • Winner earns AEW International Championship Match at AEW Battle of the Belts VIII: Kip Sabian vs. John Silver vs. Brother Xay

WrestleZone will have coverage of AEW Rampage as it airs on October 20.
