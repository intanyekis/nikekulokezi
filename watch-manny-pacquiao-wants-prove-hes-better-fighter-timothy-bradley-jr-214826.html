Manny Pacquiao Wants To Prove Hes A Better Fighter Than Timothy Bradley Jr.

The anticipation is continuing to build for the upcoming rematch fight between Manny Pacquiao and Timothy Bradley Jr,. Both men are ready to lace up their gloves, put in their mouth pieces, and enter the ring to see who will come out with a victory.

“Because I am facing Tim Bradley again I am extra motivated for this fight.,” says Pacquiao. “I may not have won the decision the first time we fought but I know I did not lose that fight. I want the world title he won from me back around my waist. I want to prove I am the better fighter.”

Pacquiao and Bradley both are eager and determined for this rematch to be the fight of the year. Although Bradley did not receive much praise for his victory against Pacquiao back in 2012, Bradley is sure to let everyone know that he’s worked hard for the last fight and will work even harder this time around for a chance at redemption.

“Although I am certain I won the fight it is now time to once again show the world who Timothy Bradley really is, ” says Bradley. “On April 12th, we will settle for once and for all the uncertainty of boxing followers and those of the fans.”

Manny Pacquiao and Timothy Bradley Jr. meet face-to-face outside the ring for this exclusive interview with Max Kellerman. Pacquiao vs. Bradley happens Sat., April 12ht live on pay-per-view.
