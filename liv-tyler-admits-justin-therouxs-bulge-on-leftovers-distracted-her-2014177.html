Liv Tyler Admits Justin Theroux's Bulge on Leftovers Distracted Her

L-O-Liv! Actress Liv Tyler appeared on Andy Cohen‘s Watch What Happens Live! on Wednesday, July 16, where she hilariously confessed that costar Justin Theroux‘s bulge was distracting on The Leftovers.

“There was this scene where he was jogging,” Tyler, 37, told Cohen, “and there was quite a large bulge in his sweatpants and it was all over.”

Theroux, 42, shot the anatomically-revealing scene in June 2013 while working on his TV movie in Queens, NYC. Photographers caught wind of Jennifer Aniston‘s hunky fiance running commando—in a sweat-stained gray shirt and matching sweatpants—with his impressive, ahem, assets on display. (At the time, the snapshot and resulting story got plenty of clicks at Us Weekly.)

“I get distracted when I watch the pilot, definitely,” Tyler continued. “I’m like, ‘Can’t help but look there.’ But I’ve never noticed the bulge other times.”
