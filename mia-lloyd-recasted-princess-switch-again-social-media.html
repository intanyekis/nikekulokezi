Mia Lloyd on The Princess Switch sequel

Mia Lloyd is appearing in the sequel of The Princess Switch movie The Princess Switch: Switched Again. She is playing the role of Olivia. The movie was released on 19th November 2020.

Other actors in the movies are Vanessa Hudgens, Sam Palladio, Nick Sagar, Suanne Braun, Mark Fleischmann, Ricky Norwood, Florence Hall, Lachlan Nieboer, Rose Mclver, and Ben Lamb.

Mia is playing the role of Kevin’s daughter. Lloyd was not in the first part of the movie The Princess Switch but she replaced Alexa Adeosun from the previous The Princess Switch.

 It is not known why Alexa did not appear in the second part of the movie. It is not clear whether the production hired her to make the character appear older or if Alexa was not available.

The character Olivia played a rather significant role in helping Kevin and Stacy find love and the follow-up sees her continue to do much of the same as a pre-teen.

In the second part of the film, viewers learn that  Margaret and Kevin have broken up because of the pressures she is feeling regarding her royal duties. Olivia tries to bring her dad and Margaret together once again.

However, fans are not quite happy with the recast of Olivia’s character. Some Princess Switch fans have made a tweet saying they are not happy with the casting.

One of the users said,

“It’s a shame @netflix why did you guys recast Olivia?! I missed her in The Princess Switch: Switched Again. I hate it when ya’ll do this.”
