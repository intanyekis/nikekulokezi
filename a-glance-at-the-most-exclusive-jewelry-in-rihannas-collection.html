A Glance At The Most Exclusive Jewelry In Rihanna's Collection

Riri's fashion sense has been popular ever since she entered the music world and brought a new wave of fans with her head-bopping tunes. Although the star considers her mother a fashionista, Rihanna’s glamorous red carpet looks and jewelry have her a fashion icon. She brings a flare of her unapologetic Caribbean roots to her jewelry through her multicolored collection, which gained a lot of traction and became popular worldwide.

From a vintage Cartier necklace from the 1930s to wearing matching multicolored and aquamarine jewels, the artist shines bright like a diamond with her glittering pieces. Riri's collection does not go unnoticed at exclusive events, be it red carpet appearances to the Met Gala or the Grammys.

Matching Aquamarine Necklace

Rihanna stunned everyone at the red carpet during the 2019 British Fashion Awards, where her brand Fenty was honored with an award. Dressed in a blue-mint dress, she matched her outfit with glittering jewels. According to Vogue, she matched her outfit with a Boghossian Silk Mardin necklace made from 21 carats of diamonds and 37 carats of aquamarines. Additionally, she also wore a 13.35-carat aquamarine and 3.48-carat Paraiba studded Boghossian Kissing ring set.

Bulgari Diamond Necklace

The singer has slayed the red carpet at Met Gala with her outfits, and 2021’s event was no different. Dressed in a Couture Balenciaga, she gave one of her most memorable looks. Along with her dress, her beautiful jewels too caught everyone’s attention. The artist was wearing 267 carats worth of Bulgari jewelry. Her neck adored a Bulgari high jewelry necklace in platinum and diamond and a heritage necklace with pear-shaped earrings.

Archival Cartier Necklace

Nominated as the co-chair for the Met Gala alongside Anna Wintour, the artist showed one of her best fashion moments at the 2018 Met Gala. Her outfit was a pearl and jewel-encrusted robe matched with jewelry from Cartier and Maria Tash Jewels, as noted by PopSugar. The necklace was from Cartier’s 1930s archival collection, and the original diamonds were worth $33,000 in 1934.

Multicolored Chopard Earrings

The singer stepped on the carpet fashionably late, dressed in a custom Armani Privé. Due to her collaboration, the appearance got completed with jewelry from Chopard. In 2017, she also launched Rihanna Loves Chopard Haute Joaillerie and used the jewels from the same collection. She wore a pair of multicolored chandelier earrings and a solitaire ring at the Grammys.

L’Heure du Diamant Earrings

Hosting the fourth Annual Diamond Ball, the diva did not disappoint with her look. Along with her stunning white jumpsuit, she wore an astonishing pair of earrings. The Chopard pair were 50-carat cushion-cut earrings, and a diamond hoop earring set taken from the L’Heure du Diamant Collection.

Diamonds And Cuffs

Rihanna wore a curation of different jewelers at the 2019 Queen And Slim Premiere. She paired her black outfit with an eye-catching David Webb necklace. Made from diamond, black enamel, carved crystal rock, and gold the necklace was remarkable. She layered it with a Neil Lane diamond and onyx chain. She gave it a touch with fluted crystal cufflinks that cost a whopping $72,000.

Rihanna Loves Chopard Set

In her signature style, the artist concluded the red carpet during the 2017 Met Gala. Her outfit matched with jewelry from the Rihanna Loves Chopard collaboration and featured floral earrings set in 27.78-carat of diamond beads, 1.80-carat of rubellites, 1.33-carat of black diamonds, 34.89-carat of tourmaline briolettes all set in 18-carat white gold, as noted by Forbes. She paired the jewelry with an 18-carat white gold floral watch.

Nouvelle Vague Necklace

Rihanna set the red carpet ablaze with her exotic outfit at the 2015 Met Gala, dressed in a yellow fur-trimmed gown from Guo Pei. She paired the handmade outfit with vintage jewelry from Cartier. Along with a golden headpiece, she wore a Nouvelle Vague Necklace by Cartier set in 18-carat rose gold and featured multiple strands.

Rihanna’s Million Dollar Birthday Jewelry

She celebrated her 30th birthday and went all out to make the party memorable for herself and the fans. Dressed to the nines, she paired her outfit with jewelry worth $1 million and wore a pair of Verdura earrings made from pink topaz and matching ear clips used as brooches in 1951. She wore Oscar Heyman diamond bracelets worth $350,000 and $200,000.

Mix-And-Match Look

Dressed in a custom Molly Goddard, the singer paired her outfit with a diamond and earrings set from Chopard at the 2017 Fenty launch. She also wore a ring by Anabel Chang, a sustainable jewelry designer. Her diamond rings were by Messika, and ear cuffs got designed by Anita Ko, a Los-Angeles based jeweler, as reported by Harper’s Bazaar.

A believer in creating and reinventing, Rihanna has a brilliant style inspired by her roots and culture. Through her fashion brand Fenty Beauty, Rihanna launched her jewelry collection called Fenty Cameo in 2019 to celebrate the Beauty Of Black Women, which depicted the history of their culture.
