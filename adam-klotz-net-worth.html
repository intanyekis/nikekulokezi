Biography of Adam Klotz

Adam Klotz, born on January 29, 1985, in Warsaw, Indiana, United States, is an accomplished meteorologist known for his work at FOX News Channel and FOX Business Network. Raised in Warsaw, he attended Warsaw Community High School, where he developed a passion for weather and reporting.

Adam’s journey in the field of journalism continued as he pursued a B.A. in broadcast journalism from Ball State University. During his time at the university, he honed his skills and gained valuable experience through an internship at CMT (Country Music Television), graduating in 2008.

Prior to joining the FOX network, Adam Klotz made significant contributions as a weekend meteorologist and multimedia journalist for Fox 5 (WAGA-TV) in Atlanta, Georgia. Through his dedication and talent, Adam gained recognition for his accurate forecasts and compelling storytelling.

Adam Klotz’s diverse background and education have shaped his meteorological expertise and his ability to connect with viewers on a personal level. As a trusted meteorologist, he brings a unique perspective to his weather reporting, ensuring that audiences stay informed and prepared for any weather conditions.

Career of Adam Klotz

Adam Klotz first embarked on his meteorology career in Lima, Ohio, where he spent approximately a year and a half as a weatherman. From there, he transitioned to become a weather anchor for WJHG-TV NewsChannel 7. However, it was in 2014 when Klotz’s career really took off as he joined Fox 5 in Atlanta, Georgia.

Recognizing his immense potential, in January 2017 Adam Klotz was recruited by FOX News Channel and FOX Business Network to serve as a meteorologist. Over the years, Klotz has covered several significant weather events, including providing live coverage of the powerful winter storm known as Stella in 2017.

Adam Klotz has displayed his expertise and dedication throughout his career, making a name for himself in the meteorology field. His passion for weather and his commitment to keeping people informed have solidified his position as a respected meteorologist at FOX News Channel and FOX Business Network.
