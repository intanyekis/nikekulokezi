Leonardo DiCaprio And Gigi Hadid Reconcile Relationship To Have Fun After He Refused To Commit

Highlights

  • Leonardo DiCaprio and Gigi Hadid have rekindled their romance, but the relationship remains strictly casual, with open communication and occasional meetings.
  • Gigi is focused on her single life, her daughter, and her career, and does not see the situation with Leo as something she would settle down for.
  • There has been speculation that Leo's involvement with Gigi might be a publicity stunt, given his reputation for dating younger women and the controversy surrounding his previous relationship with Camila Morrone.

Months after they appeared to call it quits for good, Leonardo DiCaprio and Gigi Hadid have seemingly reignited their romance, but reports warn that the pair are strictly casual.

Nearly a year after they were first romantically connected, Leonardo DiCaprio and Gigi Hadid are maintaining open communication, but they're not committing to one another. “Leo and Gigi still talk on occasion, and they see each other fairly often because they travel in similar circles of friends,” a source told Us Weekly.

“They respect each other and have fun when they’re together, but it’s not the type of situation Gigi would settle down for,” they continued. “She’s more than happy living the single life and focusing on her daughter and her career.”

Leonardo & Gigi Broke Up After He Was Seen With Other Women

They were first linked in the summer of 2022, before officially starting to date in September. But by February, it was reported the pair broke up. “Gigi and Leo are no longer dating,” an insider said at the time. “Things just organically tapered off between them. Leo and Gigi were at different points in their life, and it just didn’t work out between the two of them.”

The same source claimed their relationship was “never serious.” “They both have incredibly busy careers and lives, and although they tried to make time for each other, it really wasn’t enough to make things work,” they explained.

However, there was speculation that Gigi, 28, wanted something more serious than Leonardo, 48, who has a notorious reputation for dating younger women, especially since he was seen hanging out with other ladies amid their romance.

Leonardo & Gigi’s Romance Was Accused Of Being A Publicity Stunt

Interestingly, there’s been speculation from the beginning that Leonardo and Gigi’s relationship is all one big publicity stunt.

Before dating Gigi, the Titanic actor was dating model Camila Morrone for years, but broke up shortly after her 25th birthday. They led to a flurry of online jokes and memes, attributing Camila's age as the cause of their split (humorously suggesting she's now "too old" for him).

Moreover, Leo faced reproach for what was deemed unsettling behavior. It resurfaced that Leo initially met Camila when she was 11 years old, introduced to him through her stepfather, Al Pacino, leading him to face allegations of grooming the much younger model into his girlfriend.

This is the very reason people speculate that Leo's involvement with Gigi could be a big PR stunt. The Victoria's Secret model is slightly older than the women Leo typically dates, as she's 28 years old.

Gigi is certainly not old, and she's widely regarded as one of the world's most stunning women. However, by pursuing a relationship with someone slightly older than his usual romantic choices, it's conceivable that Leonardo is aiming to mitigate the aftermath of his split with Camila by doing some damage control. This could also be why source claims the pair aren’t serious, yet they keep making headlines with their romance.
