The Cast Of Obi-Wan Kenobi, Ranked By Net Worth

Obi-Wan Kenobi is one of the newest series to drop on Disney+. This television show focuses on Obi-Wan Kenobi and others from his life in the Star Wars universe. Fans will see a few actors reprising their roles as well as meet new faces. Episodes are still releasing, dropping every week since May 27th. Here is the cast of Obi-Wan Kenobi ranked by their net worth.

9 Moses Ingram Has A Net Worth Of $500,000

Moses Ingram is new to Hollywood, acting in only six productions prior to Obi-Wan Kenobi. Although she doesn’t have an extensive resume yet, she has been involved in some big titles, such as the 2022 film Ambulance, The Tragedy of MacBeth, and Netflix’s original television series The Queen’s Gambit. As she is still growing in her career, her net worth is currently around $500,000.

8 Simone Kessell (Breha) Has A $1.5 Million Net Worth

Simone Kessell was hired to play Breha Organa in this TV show. She is very comfortable with television series acting and has been in several titles, including Reckoning, The Crossing, and Wonderland. Kessell has also been involved in the (non-Disney) Hercules franchise, acting in three TV movies in 1994 and the show Hercules: The Legendary Journeys. These productions have helped boost her net worth to $1.5 million.

7 Vivien Lyra Blair (Leia) Is Worth Approximately $2 Million

The youngest star on this series is Vivien Lyra Blair, who was hired to portray a young Leia. She has essentially grown up in acting, starting on Band Aid in 2017 and launching her into popular productions such as Waco, Bird Box, and most recently an episode of The First Lady. Vivien now has three projects in the works, and all of these titles have helped bring her net worth to approximately $2 million.

6 Joel Edgerton (Owen) Has A Net Worth Of $3 Million

Joel Edgerton does a little bit of everything. Besides reprising his role as Owen Lars in Obi-Wan Kenobi, he has taken part in a podcast short, produced eleven titles, wrote for thirteen projects, and directed five productions. Some of his most notable performances have been in the films The Great Gatsby, The Odd Life of Timothy Green, and The Gift. His accomplishments have added to his $3 million net worth.

5 Rupert Friend (Grand Inquisitor) Has A $4 Million Net Worth

The Anatomy of a Scandal star Rupert Friend played Grand Inquisitor. He is well known in the Hollywood sphere, having acted in many big-name titles. Some of his most popular productions include Pride & Prejudice, The Young Victoria, A Simple Favor, and The Boy in the Striped Pajamas. He is a hot talent, and his notoriety has brought his net worth to $4 million.

4 Sung Kang (Fifth Brother) Is Currently Worth $8 Million

Sung Kang has over 50 titles on his filmography, which has helped boost his net worth to $8 million. Not only is he Fifth Brother in this Star Wars series, but he has also been cast in the Fast and Furious franchise, voiced a character in Disney’s animated Raya and the Last Dragon, and Bullet to the Head. He is a prominent Korean-American actor and is currently filming Fast X.

3 Kumail Nanjiani (Haja) Has An $8 Million Net Worth

One of Hollywood’s favorite comedic actors, Kumail Nanjiani, was hired to play Haja Estree. This goofy actor was the talk of the town when training for Marvel’sEternals, as he hit the training hard and his physique changed dramatically. He has been in several movies, including Life as We Know It, Stuber, The Lego Ninjago Movie, and the 2020 film Dolittle. His net worth has risen to an impressive $8 million.

2 Hayden Christensen (Darth Vader) Is Worth $12 Million

The star of Star Wars’ episodes one through three has come back to Hollywood to reprise his role as Anakin Skywalker/Darth Vader. Prior to Obi-Wan Kenobi, Hayden Christensen’s last role was in voicing Anakin in Star Wars: Episode IX – The Rise of Skywalker in 2019. He has acted in dozens of other productions, which has helped add to his $12 million net worth.

1 Ewan McGregor (Obi-Wan Kenobi) Has A Net Worth Of $25 Million

Ewan McGregor has come back to once again take up the mantel of Obi-Wan Kenobi. Besides his iconic character in the Star Wars universe, he has been in films like Doctor Sleep, Birds of Prey, Disney’s live action Beauty in the Beast, and Moulin Rouge! His popularity and talent have raised him to have the highest net worth of the cast at $25 million.
