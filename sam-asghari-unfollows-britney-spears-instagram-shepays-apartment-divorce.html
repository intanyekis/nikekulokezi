Sam Asghari Unfollows Britney Spears On Instagram After She Agrees To Pay For His $10,000 Apartment

Highlights

  • Sam Asghari unfollows Britney Spears on social media amidst their tense divorce, while Britney continues to flaunt provocative behavior online.
  • Britney has been openly provocative since the divorce, including throwing a "divorce party" and posting videos of herself with shirtless men.
  • Britney is reportedly paying for Sam's $10,000 per month luxury apartment to avoid potential financial disputes in their ongoing divorce.

The situation between Sam Asghari and Britney Spears seems more tense than ever, as they navigate their ongoing divorce. Most recently, Sam unfollowed Britney on social media, as she continues to flaunt provocative behavior online.

As of Wednesday, August 30th, Sam was no longer listed as one of Spears’ 42 million Instagram followers, though he had been on the list earlier in the day. For now, the pop star continues to follow her ex-partner's account.

Britney Has Been Extra Provocative Online Since Their Divorce

Sam filed for divorce on August 16th amid wild speculation, including the rumor that Britney may have cheated on Sam with an employee. Rather than lay low, however, Britney has done her best to show her followers she’s doing okay, including throwing a “divorce party.”

Last week, the singer made headlines for featuring a series of shirtless men on her Instagram, while they partied together at her California mansion. In a video clip, Britney could be seen rolling around on a bed without wearing a shirt. During one moment, she extended her leg into the air to reveal nothing but her underwear and thigh-high boots.

In a separate video, the former Disney sensation had her leg humorously licked by an unidentified man. A voice from off-camera could be heard instructing, "Begin at the boot, bro." Further clips presented Britney in a translucent green mini dress, being lifted by a group of shirtless men near her outdoor pool.

While Sam hasn't provided a direct comment on Britney's so-called "divorce party," reports say the Lioness star remains resolute in his choice to pursue divorce, bolstered by Britney's recent online actions.

“Sam isn’t sure if Britney was trying to get his attention on social media by throwing a divorce party and partying with other men, but it’s none of his business,” a source told Us Weekly. “It only reinforces his feelings on the split and knowing he did the right thing.”

Britney Is Paying For Sam’s $10,000 Apartment

Sam reportedly moved out of the home he once shared with Britney (his name reportedly isn’t on the deed). He’s reportedly living in a nearby luxury apartment, and it appears Britney is footing the bill, at the recommendation of her lawyers.

The standard price for the unit is reportedly $10,000. Nevertheless, Sam apparently negotiated a more advantageous agreement, securing an apartment at a lower cost.

Given the strained nature of Sam and Britney's break-up, it may come across as unexpected that she’s covering the cost for his post-split rental. However, speaking to TMZ, insiders suggest that Britney's team views this as a strategic decision. This move is prompted by the fact that Sam's legal counsel has hinted at a possible contestation of the prenuptial agreement, a situation that could result in a costly divorce settlement.

There have been rumors that Sam wants to challenge his and Britney’s prenup, since it only entitled him to a million-dollar payout after 2 years of marriage, though he filed for divorce after only 14 months. However, Sam has denied the allegations via a statement from his representative, and the divorce remains ongoing.
