Biography

Emma Coburn was born in Crested Butte, CO on October 19, 1990. Middle distance runner known for setting an American record in the 3000 meter steeplechase at the 2016 Olympic Games. She also won the United States National Championship in the 3000 meter five times from 2011 to 2016. In 2016, she met sportscaster Al Michaels who inspired her to go after her dream of joining the Olympics. She attended Crested Butte Community High School where she finished second in the 2008 Nike Outdoors Nationals Track and 2008 Field Championships.
