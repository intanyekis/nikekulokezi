Richard Attenborough Career

Attenborough had already made a reputation for himself in Hollywood as an actor, but when he directed Gandhi, he established himself as a legend in his own right. He began his career as a theatrical actor before transitioning to film. He was a prolific actor who worked long hours and was one of the most gifted British performers of all time. Richard, the son of a scholar and academician, pursued a unique career path.

His parents' devotion to their respective occupations, as well as their idealism and sense of civic duty, he internalized.
