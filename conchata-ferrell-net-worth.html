Conchata Ferrell has an estimated net worth of about $7 million and an annual salary of about $150 thousand as at 2013. The American actress has played various characters throughout her career taking part in TV movies, theatrical films, guest spots and numerous TV series. Her career really took off when she clinched the part of the sharp-tonged snappy,housekeeper to Charlie Sheen "Berta" in the Television Sitcom "Two and a Half Men".
