Nile Rodgers Parents: Meet Beverly Goodman, Nile Rodgers, Sr.

Nile Gregory Rodgers Jr., an American musician, record producer, and songwriter, was born on September 19, 1952. As a co-founder of Chic, Rodgers has contributed lyrics, music, and production to albums and songs that have collectively sold more than 500 million copies worldwide.

He is a member of the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, a three-time Grammy Award winner, the chairman of the Songwriters Hall of Fame, and a recipient of the Recording Academy’s Lifetime Achievement Award. Rolling Stone stated in 2014 that “the whole scope of Nile Rodgers’ career is still hard to understand” despite his reputation for “chucking” guitar.

Nile Rodgers Parents: Meet Beverly Goodman, Nile Rodgers, Sr.

Beverly Goodman gave birth to Nile Gregory Rodgers Jr. on September 19, 1952 on New York City’s Lower East Side. Rodgers was born when she was 14 years old. Although he had a significant impact on his life, Nile Rodgers Sr., his real father, was rarely present when Rodgers was growing up. Before his passing in 1970, Rodgers only had a “handful” of encounters with him. Goodman wed Bobby Glanzrock in 1959; in his 2011 autobiography, Richard Rodgers referred to him as a “beatnik PhD, whose views had angles that would make Miles Davis question his cool.”
