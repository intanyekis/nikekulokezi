How Close Are Euphoria Stars, Zendaya And Hunter Schafer?

Being one of TV's biggest shows, people want to know anything and everything about the stars of Euphoria. Whether it's Barbie Ferreira's departure, how Angus Cloud approaches his character work, or something in between, people can't get enough of Euphoria's biggest stars.

Zendaya is the show's biggest name, and people have noticed that she has great chemistry on screen with Hunter Schafer. Are the two close away from set, or are they just that good at their jobs? Let's take a closer look at how the show brought them together, and if they are as close as they seem.

Euphoria Is A Smash Hit Show That Brought Zendaya And Hunter Schafer Together

Unless you haven't paid attention to major TV the last few years, then you're likely aware that Euphoria is an immensely popular show, that is bolstered by an exceptional young cast.

The show features major names like Zendaya, Sydney Sweeney, Hunter Schafer, and a lot more.

One major boost that the show has gotten, is that the actors understand their characters so well.

"There's a lot of me in Jules. I do think blurred lines between an actor and a character make a deeper character. The work of an actor is trying to simulate a full life. Some people might say that doesn’t make me as strong of an actor, but … that’s how I learned. I’m still forming that process and also setting boundaries, which I didn’t have in season one," said Schafer.

Early on, it was quite clear that many of the stars of the series had bonded behind the scenes, and given their chemistry in front of the cameras, people were curious to know how things are between Zendaya and Hunter Schafer. Turns out, the pair are extremely close when the cameras aren't rolling.

The Two Are As Thick As Thieves Outside The Show

Zendaya and Hunter Schafer are incredible friends, and early on Zendaya could see that she needed to look out for the young Schafer.

Said Zendaya, "Knowing that [Hunter] was so young and so much of this was new to her … My thing was being as much of a big sister as I could. What we do for a living is important, but the friendship we share is more important.”

This was hugely helpful to Schafer, who was learning on the fly.

"Season one was like a super intense acting course. Even in the pilot, I remember in between takes being like, ‘Oh my God, this is so f--king hard. I have no idea what I'm doing," the actress said.

She went on to say, "I was really truly learning while making an HBO television show, which is just a wild circumstance to be in… I think I'm finally at a place where I'm like, okay I like this, I can keep doing this."

Having Zendaya must have been a breath of fresh air, and the duo became close friends, thanks to spending so much time filming together.

Just like any set of friends, the pair even have a nickname for one another, according to Schafer.

"We call each other 'boob' or 'boobies. It's endearing, you know," she said.

For the experienced Zendaya, this is hardly the only co-star that she has become close friends with.

Zendaya Has Gotten Close With Numerous Castmates

"Zendaya and Zac Efron starred as love interests in The Greatest Showman, but in real life they're great friends. Efron even gushed to Vogue about how impressive Zendaya was in doing her own stunts for the movie.

"While I was wincing in pain and covering myself in Tiger Balm and KT tape, Zendaya would move to her next scene completely unfazed," Efron said. He shared a photo of the pair while promoting The Greatest Showman, dubbing them "Z squared,'" Ranker wrote.

Zendaya has also had public friendships with co-stars like Timothee Chalamet, Jacob Batalon, and plenty more.

Zendaya has also found love with one of her co-stars.

In keeping with the Spider-Man actor falls for his leading lady, MCU's Tom Holland has been with Zendaya for years now.

The two kept things under wraps for quite some time, but eventually, their relationship came to light. Since then, they have been far more public with things, even posting on social media about one another.

That said, they still try to be as private as possible.

Said Holland, "Our relationship is something that we are incredibly protective of and we want to keep as sacred as possible. We don’t think that we owe it to anyone, it’s our thing, and it has nothing to do with our care."
