What is Jennie Garth’s Net Worth and Salary?

Jennie Garth, the American actress, has a net worth of $8 million. She is best known for her roles as Kelly Taylor on “Beverly Hills, 90210” and Valerie Tyler on “What I Like About You”.

Garth reprised her role on “90210” and earned $50,000 per episode. She also competed on “Dancing with the Stars” and came in fourth place. Additionally, she starred in a CMT reality show called “Jennie Garth: A Little Bit Country” and published a memoir titled “Deep Thoughts From a Hollywood Blonde” in 2014.

Jennie Garth’s Early Life and Career

Jennie Garth was born in Urbana, Illinois, in 1972 and spent some of her childhood on a horse ranch before moving to Glendale, Arizona, at age 13. She took dance classes and began modeling before being discovered by Hollywood scout Randy James and dropping out of high school to pursue an acting career in Los Angeles.

Garth eventually received her diploma in California.

Jennie Garth’s Career Highlights

In 1989, Garth appeared on “Growing Pains,” “Teen Angel Returns,” and “Brand New Life.” She gained worldwide fame as Kelly Taylor on “Beverly Hills, 90210,” starring in all 292 episodes and directing two. During her time on the show, Garth appeared on “Parker Lewis Can’t Lose,” “The Larry Sanders Show,” and “Melrose Place,” as well as in several made-for-TV movies. After “90210,” she had a recurring role on “The $treet” and starred on “What I Like About You” for four seasons. Garth also participated in reality TV projects and launched an online subscription box and a jewelry collection.

Marriages and Health

Jennie Garth was first married to musician Daniel B. Clark from 1994 to 1996. She then married actor Peter Facinelli in 2001, with whom she had three daughters before their divorce in 2013. Garth converted to Catholicism for her marriage to Facinelli, but no longer considers herself Catholic. In 2015, she married actor David Abrams, who filed for divorce in 2018 but later reconciled with Garth. She suffers from a leaky heart valve, which she was diagnosed with in 2002.

Jennie Garth’s Awards and Nominations

Jennie Garth was nominated for a 1991 Young Artist Award for Best Young Actress Supporting or Re-Occurring Role for a TV Series and won the 1992 award for Best Young Actress Co-starring in a Television Series for her role on “Beverly Hills, 90210.” The show also earned her three Bravo Otto nominations (winning in 1994) as well as a Kids’ Choice Award nomination.

In 2008, Garth won a Prism Award for Outstanding Performance in a Miniseries for “Girl, Positive” and shared a Camie with her “Secret Santa” castmates at the Character and Morality in Entertainment Awards in 2005.

Jennie Garth’s Real Estate Ventures

In 2013, Jennie Garth purchased Kathy Najimy’s 4,100 square foot home in Los Angeles for $2 million. The property was renovated for her 2014 HGTV show, “The Jennie Garth Project” and was later sold for $4.4775 million in 2018.

In 2019, Garth sold her Los Olivos ranch for $1.9 million, before reportedly purchasing a 4-bedroom home in Pasadena for $3.3 million.
