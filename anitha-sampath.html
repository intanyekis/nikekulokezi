Biography Timeline

Anitha Sampath is a prominent journalist, newsreader, and television presenter from India. She is best known for her work as a news anchor and journalist for Puthiya Thalaimurai TV. She gained immense popularity for her in-depth reporting on current affairs, politics, and social issues. Her journey in the media industry has been remarkable, and here is a timeline of some of the most significant events in her life and career:
  • June 12, 1992: Anitha Sampath was born in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India.
  • 2010: Anitha Sampath graduated from Anna University with an Electrical and Electronics Engineering degree.
  • 2011-2013: She worked as an engineer at Infosys Limited for two years.
  • 2013-2016: She pursued a Master’s in Journalism and Mass Communication from the Asian College of Journalism, Chennai.
  • 2016-2019: Anitha Sampath worked as a journalist and newsreader at Puthiya Thalaimurai TV, where she became famous for her shows like ‘Kelvi Neram’ and ‘Nerpada Pesu.’
  • 2020: She participated in the popular reality show Bigg Boss Tamil Season 4 and finished fifth place.
  • 2021: Anitha Sampath left Puthiya Thalaimurai TV to pursue new opportunities and joined Sun Network’s News18 Tamil Nadu as a news anchor and journalist.
Anitha Sampath’s career has been characterized by her dedication to journalism and delivering unbiased news to her viewers.
