Melania Trump's Mood Visibly Changed After Anderson Cooper Brought Up Donald Trump's Rumored Infidel

Highlights

  • Melania directly addressed allegations of Donald Trump's sexual misconduct, asserting her ability to handle everything and not needing pity.
  • Fans praised Melania for her strength, intelligence, and her ability to speak candidly about sensitive topics.
  • Melania revealed that she doesn't always agree with Donald Trump, emphasizing their independence as individuals in their relationship.

In the past, Melania has revealed the truth about her marriage multiple times during interviews. She hasn't backed down from the questions regarding Donald Trump, and that was especially true during an interview alongside Anderson Cooper, one that took place in her own home.

In the following, we're going to take a closer look back at the interview, and how Melania handled a question regarding Trump's "sexual misconduct." Fans noticed that Melania's mood instantly changed when the matter was brought up by host Anderson Cooper.

Let's take a look back at what took place, and how fans reacted to it all.

Melania Completely Denied The Claims Of Donald Trump's Misconduct Allegations

The interview took place on Anderson Cooper 360. The host took on the interview at the couple's apartment at the Trump Tower. He was given great access. However, Cooper was still asking the hard-hitting questions. Among them were the allegations that were surfacing at the time related to Trump's apparent sexual misconduct in the past.

When the question is asked, Melania's facial expressions clearly take a turn. Melania's message was direct, telling people not to worry about her, she's able to hold her own.

Melania said, “I’m very strong. And people, they don’t really know me. People think I talk about me like, ‘Oh Melania. Oh poor Melania.’ Don’t feel sorry for me. I can handle everything.”

She went on to take aim at other celebrities that were egging on the allegations at the time.

“I see in the press a lot almost like celebrities or people who think they’re celebrity. I would suggest to them at look at themselves in the mirror and to look at their actions and to take care of their own families,” she said.

Fans had a lot to say looking back at the interview and, for the most part, Melania received a lot of support for speaking her mind in regards to such a sensitive topic at the time.

The interview that took place seven years ago is definitely worth a look back on.

Melania Spoke Her Mind Revealing She Doesn't Always Agree With What Donald Trump Has To Say

Melania made it clear in the past, she doesn't always agree with Donald Trump. Nonetheless, Melania doesn't want to change Trump as both are separate individuals. Melania revealed, “We are both very independent – I let him be who he is and he let me be who I am,” she said.

“I don’t try to change him. He’s an adult. He knows the consequences and so I let him be who he is. I give him my opinions, many, many times.” She added: “I don’t agree with everything that he says, but you know, that is normal. I’m my own person, I tell him what I think, I’m standing very strong on the ground on my two feet and I’m my own person, and I think that’s very important in the relationship.”
