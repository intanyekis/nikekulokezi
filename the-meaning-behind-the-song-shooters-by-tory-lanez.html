The Meaning Behind The Song: Shooters by Tory Lanez

Shooters by Tory Lanez, released in 2017, is a banger that showcases Lanez’s skill as a rapper and his love for luxury and wealth. The song is the first single off his sophomore album, MEMORIES DON’T DIE. It features lyrics that boast about his wrist game, his success in the music industry, and his desire to “go out trappin’ with the shooters”. The catchy chorus and hard-hitting beats make this track a favorite among fans.

However, beyond the surface-level lyrics about wealth and guns, the music video for Shooters delves into a more conscious issue – police brutality. In the intro of the video, Tory Lanez makes it clear that he does not endorse violence or brutality. He believes that in times of duress, it is important for the community to share their fears and concerns to support one another better.

The video follows Tory Lanez and an accomplice seeking revenge on a police officer who shot his cousin. However, in a tragic turn of events, both of them are shot down in cold blood by the police. This powerful visual representation sheds light on the real-life consequences of police brutality and the ongoing issues faced by marginalized communities.

I personally resonate with the message conveyed in Shooters. Growing up in a neighborhood that faced its fair share of police harassment, I understand the fear and frustration that comes with living in a community where law enforcement can be unjust and abusive. The video’s portrayal of the harsh reality faced by those affected by police brutality emphasizes the need for change and justice.

In an interview, Tory Lanez revealed that the song was initially supposed to feature Nicki Minaj. However, due to a misunderstanding during a text conversation about her verse, she asked for it to be pulled from the track. This version, leaked in 2018, showcased the collaborative talent of both artists and generated anticipation among fans.

The lyrics of Shooters may appear to glorify wealth and guns, but it is important to view this song beyond the surface level. It serves as a commentary on the realities of police brutality in marginalized communities and the need for change. Tory Lanez’s intention to spark discussions and create awareness is evident in the music video’s powerful imagery.

Shooters is a reminder that music can be a powerful tool for self-expression and social commentary. It allows artists like Tory Lanez to shed light on important issues and start conversations that can lead to change. As a fan, I appreciate artists who use their platform to address social issues and bring attention to matters that need our collective focus and action.

The meaning behind Shooters extends beyond the catchy beats and bars. It serves as a reminder of the ongoing fight against injustice and the importance of community support. Through his music and visuals, Tory Lanez sparks conversations and encourages us all to stand up against police brutality and advocate for a more just and equal society.
