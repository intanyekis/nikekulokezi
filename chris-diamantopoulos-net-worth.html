Chris Diamantopoulos Wiki Biography

Christopher Diamantopoulos was born on 9 May 1975, in Toronto, Ontario Canada, and is of Greek descent. Chris is an actor and comedian, known from his appearances in numerous popular television shows and stage plays, including “Charmed”, “Boston Legal”, and “The Office”. All of his efforts have helped put his net worth to where it is today.

How rich is Chris Diamantopoulos? As of mid-2017, sources estimate a net worth that is at $2 million, mostly earned through a successful career in acting which began as a child actor. He also does voice acting work, well-known to voice Mickey Mouse and various “Batman” animated projects. As he continues his career, it is expected that his wealth will also continue to increase.

Chris Diamantopoulos Net Worth $2 million

Chris started his career appearing in professional theatre productions when he was nine years old. He also had several television appearances in commercials. When he turned 18, he left home to become a part of US national tours, and then found work on Broadway, becoming the lead of popular plays such as “The Full Monty” and “Les Miserables”. He also started studying at East York Collegiate Institute.

Diamantopoulos made guest appearances in various popular shows, including “Kevin Hill”, “Nip/Tuck” and “The Sopranos”. All of these opportunities helped increase his net worth. He portrayed Robin Williams in “Behind the Camera: The Unauthorized Story of Mork & Mindy”, and in 2007, he played the role of gay interior decorator Rodney in “The Starter Wife”. In 2009, he ventured into voice work, making a guest contribution in “American Dad!” He also had a recurring role in “State of Mind” which was aired on Lifetime, and then joined the eighth season of “24” in which he played the Chief of Staff Rob Weiss, and afterwards appeared in “The Kennedys” playing Frank Sinatra. His net worth was rising steadily.

In 2012, Chris was cast in “The Three Stooges” film in which he played Moe Howard, and became the understudy of Donny Osmond in the tour of “Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat”. He continued having recurring roles in shows such as “The Office” in which he played a former member of the documentary film crew Brian. He was also a part of “Up All Night” and “Arrested Development” playing face-blind environmentalist Marky. He then voiced Mickey Mouse for the animated series of the same name. His net worth continued to build thanks to these numerous opportunities.

A few of his latest projects include “Episodes” playing TV network boss Castor Sotto. He also played Mr. Chris in the television adaptation of “About a Boy”, and was cast in “Silicon Valley” as billionaire Russ Hanneman, aired as part of HBO. He then starred in the Broadway production of “Waitress”.

For his personal life, it is known that Diamantopoulos married actress Becki Newton in 2005, and they have two children. The couple met while at a subway station in New York. Chris spends time in between Canada and Greece. He is also a practicing member of the Greek Orthodox Church.
