Lori Harvey Denies Diddy Engagement Rumors

Someone started a rumor on Al Gore’s Internet that 22-year-old Lori Harvey was engaged to music mogul Diddy, and as ridiculous as it sounds, people ran with it.

Harvey took to her Instagram Stories to laugh off the speculation, stating that she’s not engaged, and that people should stop believing blogs. Previously, Harvey was linked to Justin Combs, one of Diddy’s sons.

Steve Harvey’s stepdaughter was spotted at a Ciroc party over the weekend with a giant ring on her finger. In a photo shared to her IG, she wrote that she was “somebody’s wife.” The two have been a rumored item since March. This rumor comes just a week after Diddy’s ex, Cassie, announced that she was expecting her first child with her new beau, Alex Fine.

“You two are my greatest loves I have and will ever have,” Fine wrote on Instagram Jun. 13 accompanying a photo of himself and his lady love. “I cannot wait for the rest of our lives together and to raise a beautiful happy child in our beautiful happy life.”

Diddy congratulated the two on his own Instagram, which was met with a lukewarm response.
