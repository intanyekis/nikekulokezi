Who Is TikToks Charli DAmelio?

Charli D'Amelio has become a household name and it is all because of TikTok! The video-sharing app has since become one of the most used apps to date and it just so happens that it's allowed countless users to skyrocket to international fame and success. Charli posted her first TikTok of trendy dances during the summer of 2019, and as of March 2020, she is currently the most followed user on the app.

Not only does she have an immense following, but D'Amelio has managed to collectively gain 5 billion video views, and considering she makes quite a nice paycheck per post, it's safe to say that Charli, along with other top users, such as Jason Derulo, is making the big bucks. Whether you follow her, know of her or have heard her name, there is no denying the influence this 15-year-old has! Here is everything we know about TikToks', Charli D'Amelio.

The True Face Of TikTok

TikTok is the newest craze right now and despite it having been released well over 4 years ago, it appears as if the app is only gaining worldwide recognition now! Similarly to Vine and Musical.ly, TikTok allows users to post up to 60-second videos completing challenges, dances, ranting, or creating new memes that have allowed for countless pages to gain nationwide fame and success, one user, in particular, is none other than Charli D'Amelio.

Charli first began her TikTok career in July 2019 and as of March 2020, she's become the most followed page on the app with over 70 million followers and over 5 billion collective views. The 15-year-old grew up as a dancer, which is ultimately what led her to TikTok in the first place. Considering TikTok was formerly known as Musical.ly a lip-syncing and dancing app, it was only fitting for Charli, a dancer, to join TikTok.

How Much Is She Worth?

During the start of her TikTok take off, Charli and her older sister and fellow TikTokers, Dixie D'Amelio, was part of a group of TikTok users known as the Hype House. This house consisted of other massive TikTok names such as Lil Huddy, Addison Rae and Alex Warren to name a few. While Charli gained a lot of fame and success as a member of the Hype House, she and Dixie have since left and gone their own way.

With a page as successful as Charli's, we can only wonder how much she makes off of TikTok. Well, according to a number of sources, with a following as big as Charli's, she can easily make anywhere between $50,000 - $100,000 per post! This is usually done so through a number of sponsors and has allowed Charli to amass a whopping net worth of $4 million in under a year!
