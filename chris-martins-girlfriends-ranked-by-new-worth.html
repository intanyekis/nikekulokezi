All Of Chris Martin's Girlfriends Ranked By Net Worth

Chris Martin was born into a musical family background, as his mother taught the subject at school. During his school years, Martin formed a band to create music before he met his Coldplay members in London at the University College, where he was pursuing Ancient World Studies. After the band was formed in 1996, their first album, Parachutes, helped them launch into stardom and become a household name worldwide. Martin has won 7 Grammys with Coldplay and collaborated with notable artists like Rihanna, Jay-Z, BTS, and The Chainsmokers. The singer has amassed a massive $150 million fortune through his career in music and sold-out concerts.

Since the beginning of his career, Chris Martin has been in the public eye for his relationships. From dating singers to notable actresses, he has a history of dating accomplished women who are independent in their fields. Let’s look at all the ladies Chris Martin has dated.

Natalie Imbruglia

Natalie Imbruglia and Chris Martin dated in 2000, long before their musical careers took off. The couple remained in a relationship for one year before calling it quits. It was reported that they collaborated on multiple songs, with Martin being credited as a songwriter for her hit albums. He later admitted to regretting giving away the songs instead of using them for Coldplay. Imbruglia became famous for starring in the Australian soap opera Neighbors before her career took off. The pair have worked together on music since then and even performed at Wembley Stadium in August 2022 as a tribute to late Grease actress Olivia Newton-John, as noted by Rolling Stone.

Gwyneth Paltrow

Gwyneth Paltrow is an Oscar-winning actress who became a household name after her role as Pepper Potts in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, starring opposite Robert Downey Jr.’s Tony Stark. Alongside her acting career, she has a $250 million lifestyle empire with her company, Goop which offers advice, products, and blogs about out-of-the-box living. According to People, Paltrow met Martin backstage in 2002 at one of Coldplay’s concerts and hit it off. They got married in 2003 and welcomed their daughter the following year. Two years later, their son was born, and it wasn’t until 2014 that the couple split. They have remained close friends ever since and even vacationed together as a family on several occasions.

Jennifer Lawrence

Four months after announcing his split with Gwyneth Paltrow, Chris Martin was spotted on a date with Jennifer Lawrence in August 2014. While they were often seen together, the couple split four months after dating but got back together in 2015 for a short period before ending things for good. Lawrence has been listed as the highest-paid actress in the world for multiple years and has earned an Oscar for her stunning portrayal in The Silver Linings Playbook. Her other notable projects include Joy, American Hustle, Don’t Look Up, and Passengers.

Annabelle Wallis

Raised in Portugal, Annabelle Wallis worked in numerous short films before moving to London to pursue a career in movies. She bagged small roles in multiple films and television shows and attained worldwide fame for starring in Peaky Blinders in 2013. Her role as Grace Burgess helped her land roles in large commercial films. Not long after splitting from Jennifer Lawrence, Chris Martin was spotted on a date with Annabelle Wallis in Brooklyn in August 2015, as stated by US Weekly. Their relationship remained on and off for two years before it ended in 2017.

Kylie Minogue

After Minogue's show, Kylie Minogue and Chris Martin were seen taking a stroll on the streets of London in 2015. They were spotted getting comfortable; however, the reps later denied that they were in a relationship. Minogue is the highest-selling female Australian singer, with over 80 million sales worldwide. She has won numerous accolades in the country and also won BRIT Awards and a Grammy Award for her music. The singer also made minor appearances in movies such as Street Fighter and Moulin Rouge!

Dakota Johnson

The daughter of Melanie Griffith and Don Johnson, Dakota Johnson, grew up around movie sets since she was a child. Soon after high school, she landed supporting roles in movies and television shows. In 2014, she landed the lead role in Fifty Shades Of Grey opposite Jamie Dornan, which boosted her into stardom. She has appeared in multiple critically-acclaimed films, including The Lost Daughter, Suspiria, The Peanut Butter Falcon, and Black Mass. As mentioned by Cosmopolitan, they began dating in October 2017 and have had a steady relationship since then. Johnson and Martin are also close friends with Gwyneth Paltrow and her now-husband Brad Falchuk.

Chris Martin has been in a long and steady relationship with Dakota Johnson, and the couple has even purchased a home in California. He remains close friends with his ex-wife Gwyneth Paltrow as the couple celebrates essential milestones. Martin is on the European Leg of Coldplay’s Music Of The Spheres World Tour.
