Jet Li’s Best Movies Highlights 

Here are some of the best highlights of Jet Li’s career:

  • 1982: Jet Li’s debut movie Shaolin Temple proves to be a huge box office smash in China, launching his professional acting career.
  • 1991: Working with iconic action choreographer Yuen Woo-ping, Li gains international attention through his starring role in the Once Upon a Time in China trilogy.
  • 1998: Li’s breakthrough Hollywood role in Lethal Weapon 4 introduces him to a mainstream Western audience for the first time.
  • 2002: After a period in America, Jet Li returns to China with a starring role in the critically acclaimed Zhang Yimou film Hero.
  • 2010: Li begins his association with his second blockbuster action trilogy, this time appearing in The Expendables movies.
