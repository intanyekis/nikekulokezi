Netflix's 'Dating Around' Couples: Where Are They Now?

When it comes to reality television series, Netflix has been doing a pretty decent job with shows like Love Is Blind, which graces individuals with a platform to ease the engagement process. And since dating has proven to be strenuous and near impossible to some people, it is always a good idea to help those struggling to find love.

Dating Around is another well-implemented idea for singles to go out in a pool of several blind dates to try and find that one person who deserves a second date. This is accompanied by flirtations, failures with several awkward moments, and a series of genuine connections.

RELATED: Ranking The 10 Most Awkward Dates From Netflix's Dating Around

Dating Around is a more realistic dating show with a sort of traditional style of romance. Drinks and dinner helped them build deeper connections, and due to some contestants being easily datable, they were quick to get a match to take to the second date. However, most couples have failed to find mutual connections since the show ended. Below are the series' couples now.

8 Luke And Victoria

Luke and Victoria are featured in the first episode of the show. Luke is a gentleman with classy and appealing looks working in the real estate industry. He attracts many women but finally settles for Victoria, a blonde beauty from Massachusetts with a great sense of humor.

After the second date, the two lovebirds' life doesn't in any way suggest that they became an item. However, the two continue to be friends. There is no social media photo of them together, and Luke has only updated his fans about his new job.

7 Lex And Cory

Lex and Cory were the subjects of the third episode in the first season. Lex is a well-established designer well-known to his friends as a hard worker with classy designer outfits. However, he introduces himself as a softie, which triggers mixed reactions from Cory, making her worried.

The two fell in love, but they couldn't work out things together for a long time. According to current social media posts for the two, it's evident that they are not dating each other.

6 Mila And Charlotte

In the last episode of the first season, fans were entertained with a romantic tale between Mila and Charlotte. Mila works as an editorial makeup artist, good enough to feature on Vogue, and Charlotte is a professional party organizer. The two jobs are likely to link up, so did the two hit it off quite easily.

The two even shared an ice cream in the rain and ended the show with a kiss, but unfortunately, they are not together anymore. Each one has moved on with her life and career, and both have found love with a different partner.

5 Justin And Ann

The second season of Dating Around opens up with the story of Justin and Ann. His peers described Justin as a carefree man who would make a good boyfriend. In the show, he was one of the easily datable contestants because of his nature and personality.

Due to being an unproblematic man, his dates with the other women, Barbara, Lilly, Ashley, and Assata went incredibly well, but he chose Ann for a second date. Since the season was wrapped, the two have not revealed the developments of their relationship. However, they still follow each other on Instagram.

4 Ben And Alex

Also, never in the realm of dating, Computer Science Professor Ben went out with five women in a bid to find the love of his life. His personality made him endearing, hence creating one of the most memorable stories of the entire season of Dating Around. He had an unforgettable time with Alex, where their conversations were both deep and flirty.

Since the end of the show, the two have never disclosed their relationship, and due to their inactivity on social media platforms, it is hard to tell whether they are still together.

3 Deva And Maria

After her five blind dates, Deva was strongly attracted to Maria, and she is the one she settled for.

According to Digital Spy, the two lovers are still on good terms, though there is no proof they continued dating. Apart from Maria's "like" on Deva's Instagram post, and the romantic biking the two had, the duo has not communicated on the subject of them being together.

2 Heather And Ernesto

Of the five dinners and drinks dates that Heather went on, three were amazing and even more than one could ask for. Philanthropist Ernesto, also an executive airline salesman, emerged at the top. The two lovers continued to see each other even after the show.

Heather posted a video showing that they continued dating and claimed to have an undeniable connection. Additionally, the two have continued working their careers. Heather has continued her job as a makeup artist and even shares what she does on YouTube.

1 Demi And Zach

Demi's appearance on Dating Around focused on getting a man who wouldn't break her heart as her previous boyfriends had done. After her five blind dates with five potential male contenders, Demi ended up with Zach as her favorite.

Sadly, history was bound to repeat itself, as the two are no longer involved despite the relationship seeming to flow well on the series. TheCinemaholic published an interview with Zach, who claims they grew apart and are not together. Also, Demi has moved on with another man, signifying that her relationship with Zach hit a dead end.
