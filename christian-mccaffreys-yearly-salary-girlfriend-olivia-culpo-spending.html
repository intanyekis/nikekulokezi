What Is Christian McCaffrey Paid Annually To Fund His Lifestyle With Olivia Culpo?

Christian McCaffrey isn't the first professional athlete Olivia Culpo has dated, but he's the first one to have recently signed a contract that makes him the NFL's highest-paid running back. According to Yahoo Life, McCaffrey signed a four-year, $64 million contract extension with the Carolina Panthers, which will go through to 2025. However, in 2022, he was traded to the San Francisco 49ers, and renegotiated his deal in 2023, placing his salary for 2024 at $11.8 million and $12 million for 2025.

Christian McCaffrey's annual salary for 2024 alone is more than Culpo's entire net worth, which is believed to be around $9 million. Despite being a few years older than McCaffrey (27), Culpo (31) had agreed to make things official with the young NFL star after the couple got engaged in April 2023. This definitely wasn't a rushed decision, as the couple had been dating for four years prior, according to People.

How Does Christian McCaffrey Spend His NFL Salary?

Beginning with one of the biggest investments of his life, Christian McCaffrey bought an engagement ring for Olivia Culpo that cost over half a million dollars. Although it won't make the list of the most expensive celebrity engagement rings ever purchased, it is definitely a pretty penny compared to what the average American spends. According to The Richest, McCaffrey designed the ring himself - "a brilliant-cut oval 8-carat center stone diamond ring accompanied with Cadillac or Epaulette diamond side stones in a trilogy design set in platinum." The exact price of the engagement ring is registered at over $621,000.

The diamond-studded ring is just the tip of the iceberg when it comes to the life of luxury that Christian McCaffrey will be sharing with Olivia Culpo. He has also been known for making lucrative real estate investments. Celebrity Net Worth tells us that he bought a condo in Charlotte, North Carolina in 2019 for $2 million, then sold it for $3 million in 2023. In 2020, he purchased another property in North Carolina for $7.5 million and sold it for $12.5 million in 2024.

Real estate isn't the only way Christian McCaffery puts his money to work for him in order to maintain the standard of living for himself and Olivia Culpo. He has also been praised for his strategic investments in companies like Pair Eyewear, which specializes in children's eyewear, and Hyperice, which provides technology for athlete recovery. Both of his investments have yielded lucrative returns, which highlights McCaffrey's business-savvy mindset when it comes to wealth accumulation and long-term financial security.
