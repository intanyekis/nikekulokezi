Terry Bradshaw is a living legend. The man secured four Superbowl wins during his NFL career and transitioned seamlessly into sportscasting once his esteemed football career was behind him. His contagious smile, charming personality, and all-around good guy attitude make him an easy guy to root for. Television & movie executives were smart enough to see these qualities in Terry, too.Soon enough he became a featured actor and a reality tv star. His family’s reality show on E!, The Bradshaw Bunch, documents his illustrious life with his wife Tammy and their three daughters.Related: This Is What Fans Really Think About The Bradshaw BunchWhat some viewers might not have known before the show, however, is just how many wives Terry has had. While Terry doesn’t often speak about his ex-wives, he was previously married to three incredibly smart and successful women. They’ve gone on to lead lives full of fortune, as well.

Terry Bradshaw’s First Wife Melissa Babish Has A Net Worth Of $1 Million

Terry married his first wife, Melissa Babish, in 1972. He was early into his NFL career, and she had just come off the heels of winning Miss Teenage America in 1970. The marriage only lasted a year.

There’s really not much known about Melissa Babish. After their divorce, she went dark. According to online sources, she has an estimated net worth of $1 million.

When asked about his first marriage, Terry doesn’t speak in detail about his relationship with Melissa or what life was like behind closed doors. But he has been candid about his own wrongdoings in the relationship.

In an interview with Sports Illustrated, Terry admitted to feeling guilty about the divorce and being moody during the relationship. A devout Christian, Terry admitted he had strayed from his values during that time, drinking heavily and flirting with women. He found footing in his faith again in the aftermath of the relationship.

What's Terry Bradshaw’s Second Wife, Angelica "Jojo" Starbuck's Net Worth?

Despite his first marriage’s quick demise, Terry wasn’t turned off by love. In fact, he embraced finding the right person for him. Three years after his divorce he was ready to marry Alicia “Jojo” Starbuck. An Olympic figure skater, the two bonded about their deep love of sport, marrying in 1976.

Jojo and Terry appeared in public quite often together, going on talk shows and to award ceremonies together. The press ate up their relationship, a gorgeous pair of impressive athletes. However, the athletic power couple’s relationship was ill-fated, divorcing in 1983. It seemed the picture-perfect couple had hidden the cracks in the foundation of their relationship from the public.

Jojo’s filing for divorce caught Terry off guard. Sources told the Washington Post that Jojo said their relationship was floundering, with Terry agreeing with her and encouraging her to come home and be with him in order to make it work. But that was just the thing, Jojo wasn’t the stay-at-home type. She loved to skate, and she needed to do it, for it to be a part of her life. To this day, at 70 years old she still laces up and gets out on the ice.

Jojo made a beautiful life for herself after her divorce from Terry. She’s been happily married to her husband, architect Jeff Gertler since 1993. She moved to Madison, New Jersey, where she teaches skating at the Essex Skating Club of New Jersey, and has two sons. She also had a small career in acting after her divorce. Her net worth is estimated to be around $500,000.

What's Terry Bradshaw’s Third Wife Worth?

Terry really thought his third wife, Charlotte Hopkins, was the one. The pair had actually met back when Charlotte was 17 and Terry 22. They dated for four years, but their relationship fizzled out, with Terry going to pursue his football career and Charlotte pursuing a career in family law. Fate had other plans, and the two reconnected during his divorce from Jojo. The same year his divorce was finalized, he and Charlotte were married.

The couple welcomed two beautiful daughters during their relationship, Rachel and Erin. As hard as they tried, after 16 years of marriage, the couple decided to call it quits. They’ve never opened up about the reasons behind the divorce, with the filing citing irreconcilable differences.

The two are amicable, with Charlotte even keeping Bradshaw as her last name. Fans may even be able to get a glimpse of Charlotte if season 3 of The Bradshaw Bunch gets the green light.

Charlotte continued her law career after her divorce, becoming a force of nature within the Texas law world. Currently, she’s ranked as one of the Top 100 Super Lawyers in the Dallas-Fort Worth area. She’s also graced the Top 50 Female Attorneys in Texas list numerous times.

Charlotte also broke into the writing world, working as a freelancer. Her work has been published in numerous magazines and online publications. According to her Facebook profile, she’s remarried as well. She’s made quite a name for herself, and her $2.1 million net worth speaks to it.
