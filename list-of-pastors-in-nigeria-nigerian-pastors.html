List of Pastors in Nigeria – Nigerian Pastors

The following is a List of Pastors in Nigeria – Nigerian Pastors

David Ibiyeomie

Pastor David Ibiyeomie is the founder and senior pastor of Salvation Ministries.

Taiwo Odukoya

Taiwo Odukoya is a Nigerian Pentecostal pastor and public speaker. He is the senior pastor of The Fountain of Life Church.

Daniel Kolawole Olukoya

Pastor Daniel Kolawole Olukoya the founder of Mountain of Fire and Miracles

Gabriel Olutola

Gabriel Olutola was born on 4 April 1933 in Ilesa, Osun State. He is a Nigerian pastor and President of The Apostolic Church Nigeria

Lazarus Muoka

Lazarus Muoka is among pastors in Nigeria and the founder of The Lord’s Chosen Charismatic Revival Movement.

Enoch Adeboye

Enoch Adeboye is a pastor in Nigeria and the General Overseer of Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG)

Tunde Bakare

Tunde Bakare is a Nigerian pastor of the Latter Rain Assembly. He ran as the vice president candidate of Muhammadu Buhari in the Nigerian presidential election, 2011

Bishop Mike Okonkwo

Bishop Mike Okonkw is a Nigerian Pentecostal pastor. He is the General Overseer of The Redeemed Evangelical Mission.

Bishop Wale Oke

Bishop Wale Oke is a renowned evangelist. He is the founder and presiding Bishop of The Sword of the Spirit Ministries, also known as Christ Life Church. The headquarters of the church are in Ibadan, Nigeria.

T. B. Joshua

He was born on June 12, 1963 in Arigidi, Nigeria. T. B. Joshua is a Nigerian pastor and founder of the Synagogue, Church Of All Nations – SCOAN.

William Kumuyi

William Kumuyi is a Nigerian pastor, evangelist, General-Overseer and founder of Deeper Christian Life Ministry. Pastor William Kumuyi was born 6 June 1941.

Chris Okotie

Chris Okotie was born on 16 June 1958. Pastor Chris Okotie is a Nigerian pastor, televangelist and founder and the General Overseer of the Household of God Church.

Pastor Chris (Chris Oyakhilome)

Pastor Chris Oyakhilome is a Nigerian pastor, evangelist who is the founding president of Believer’s Love-world also known as Christ Embassy

List of Pastors in Nigeria – Nigerian Pastors

David Oyedepo

David Oyedepo was born on September 27, 1954. He is among the many pastors in Nigeria, a Christian author, preacher, and the founder of Winners’ Chapel

Matthew Ashimolowo

Matthew Ashimolowo is the Senior Pastor of Kingsway International Christian Centre. He was born on 17 March 1952 in Nigeria.

Moses Orimolade

Moses Orimolade is a pastor and founder of Eternal Sacred Order of Cherubim and Seraphim. He was born in 1879.

Ayo Oritsejafor

Ayo Oritsejafor is the founding and Senior Pastor of Word of Life Bible Church and the former President of the Christian Association of Nigeria

Jeremiah Omoto Fufeyin

Jeremiah Omoto Fufeyin is the founder of Christ Mercyland Deliverance Ministry.

Elijah Abina

Elijah Abina is the General Overseer of The Gospel Faith Mission International (GOFAMINT)

Bola Odeleke

Bola Odeleke is the General Overseer of Power Pentecostal Church

Done P. Dabale

Done P. Dabale is the founder of the United Methodist Church in Nigeria (UMCN).

Apostle Suleman Johnson

Apostle Suleman Johnson is the founder and senior pastor of Omega Fire Ministry

Lawrence Onochie

Lawrence Onochie is a Nigerian pastor and the General Overseer of The Kings Heritage Church.

Sam Adeyemi

Sam Adeyemi is a Nigerian pastor, televangelist and Senior Pastor of Daystar Christian Centre.

Godman Akinlabi

Godman Akinlabi is the founder and a senior pastor of The Elevation Church.
