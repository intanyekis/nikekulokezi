Every year, more LGBT characters are included in television than ever before. So many of these characters have become fan favourites across borders of gender and preference. These characters are played by some of the world’s best actors and actresses, and portrayed so well that it’s impossible to tell the real orientation of the actors behind them! Here’s a list of the true orientations of the actors behind some of the most favourite LGBT characters from TV.
