Restaurants Owned by Bruce Comisar

King Cole

This restaurant, which originally opened its doors in Indianapolis and later expanded to Dayton, became a beloved destination for food enthusiasts seeking a memorable dining experience. Yankee Tavern

Another establishment that showcased Bruce Comisar’s entrepreneurial spirit was the Yankee Tavern in Centerville. Whether it was a casual gathering with friends or a special occasion, patrons could always count on the Yankee Tavern to provide a delightful dining experience that would leave a lasting impression.

Lincoln Park Grill

The Lincoln Park Grill in Kettering was a testament to Bruce Comisar’s commitment to culinary excellence. Known for its vibrant atmosphere and delectable menu, this restaurant quickly became a favorite among locals. With live music on Fridays and Saturdays, the Lincoln Park Grill offered not only a memorable dining experience but also a lively entertainment venue. Closure of Lincoln Park Grill
