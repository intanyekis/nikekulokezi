Sebastian Roche Returns To General Hospital As Jerry Jacks

document.write("");

He’s back.

A rep for ABC has confirmed to Soaps.com that Sebastian Roche is bringing his alter ego Jerry Jacks back to Port Charles. His first airdate will be December 13.

There’s no word on what will bring him back to town, but with The Balkan wreaking havoc for both Lucky (Jonathan Jackson) and Brenda (Vanessa Marcil Giovinazzo), could he be turn out to be one of Jerry’s many identities? Will he return to continue his battle with Jason (Steve Burton) and Sam (Kelly Monaco)? Or does he just want to reconnect with his brother Jax (Ingo Rademacher)? We don’t know just yet, but feel free to share your theories below.
